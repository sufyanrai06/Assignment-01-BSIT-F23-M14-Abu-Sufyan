About

Punjab University, established in 1882, is one of Pakistan’s oldest and most respected universities. It offers a wide range of programs in science, technology, business, arts, and social sciences. The university is committed to providing quality education, fostering research, and developing graduates who can contribute positively to society. This website provides information about Punjab University and its campuses, including the Main Campus in Lahore, Allama Iqbal Campus, Quaid-e-Azam Campus, Gujranwala Campus, Pothohar Campus in Rawalpindi, Khanspur Campus, and Jhelum Campus. It highlights each campus’s location, programs, and significance. The site also features pioneer batch students, such as those from the Bachelor of Information Technology (BIT) program at Pothohar Campus, demonstrating the university’s commitment to nurturing early cohorts and academic excellence. The purpose of this website is to serve as an educational resource for students, researchers, and the general public. By centralizing information about campuses, programs, and notable students, it provides easy access to reliable details about Punjab University. The website aims to promote awareness of the university’s legacy, current offerings, and educational vision, supporting learning and research across its campuses.
